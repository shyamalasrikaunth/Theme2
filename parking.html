Smart Parking Solutions

Parking Solutions

Hassle free parking solution for your car and bike with smart technolgies equipped with QR codes and smart cards.

Vehicle owners in the national capital Delhi will now be able to book parking slots via a mobile application in areas that fall under the South Delhi Municipal Corporation (SDMC).

Currently all the parking slots are managed by contractors who collect the parking charges in Cash. However with a view to support digital India campaign, SDMC has awarded contract for digitalisation of parking spaces, where Arraycom India Limited is one of the implementation partners.
